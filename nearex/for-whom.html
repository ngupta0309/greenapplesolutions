End Users

Credit and debit card users in the developed world have been enjoying cashless access to their funds for all kinds of daily payment needs. Most of the population across emerging economies have been excluded from such convenience.

XipTM Micropayment system will help extend the benefits of financial inclusion to this large base of consumers by catalysing the creation of a large scale merchant network that is ready to accept small cashless payments.

Mobile Money
Service Providers

The consumer spend in emerging markets is around USD 15 Trillion now and is expected to grow to USD 30 Trillion by 2025. Most of this money is being spent in cash.

Based on the popular merchant commission models published by some MMSPs, this spend would translate to an annual potential commission revenue of USD 150 Billion annually for the MMSPs.

XipTM Micropayment system can help unlock this opportunity in your region with a limited phased upfront investment. Most frequent micropayments are transactions performed within a regional community. Thus, a trial in a limited region could be a very good representation of the service wide success.

Please contact Nearex for further details on how to expand your Mobile Money business to include the micropayment universe in a phased approach.
