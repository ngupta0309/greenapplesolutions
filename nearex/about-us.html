Background

 

Nearex is a high-tech startup incorporated and headquartered in Singapore.

Mobile Phone penetration is reaching the remotest corners of the planet. Many conventional transactions are quickly migrating to the mobile world due the near universal access and affordability. Most emerging solutions are primarily addressing remote transactions. Nearex founding team has realized that there is immense potential to use the unlimited universal mobile connectivity to simplify proximity transactions as well.

Nearex was founded and is managed by a team of mobile and financial industry veterans.

The founders include senior executives from leading mobile money platform companies with first-hand role in success of mobile money in regions such as Africa. They bring experience in technology as well as market development domains across emerging world.

Nearex aims to empower people by simplifying proximity transactions such as micropayments.

Investors

Nearex is a privately held company with investment from individuals and industry. It is managed by a professional board.

Early stage investors include veterans with past and present roles in leading global banks. Recent investment was made by Beenos, a Japanese listed entity with extensive payment industry experience.

Partners

Nearex is establishing partnership agreements with leading mobile money platform companies.
