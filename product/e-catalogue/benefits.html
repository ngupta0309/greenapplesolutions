Product Catalogue App Benefits

The product catalogue app helps save costs by eliminating paper based catalogues, reduce effort in introducing new products thereby reducing go-to-market time considerably, helps your sales team showcase your products to clients easily; and helps you track orders and complaints.

Impress your clients

  • Get a visually-appealing electronic product catalogue with high-impact, glossy images.
  • Present product information to clients easily.
  • Zoom in on product images for high definition detail.
  • Generate and send up-to-date PDF product catalogues easily.

Save time and money

  • No more printing costs. No more embarrassing printing errors.
  • Eliminate paper usage and save the environment.
  • Easily update and maintain your catalogue yourself.
  • Sync product updates with your team instantly.

Close deals faster

  • Allow your sales staff to close deals on-site with clients.
  • Send quotes and orders to your clients quickly and easily.
  • No more delays in processing new orders.

Real-time sales tracking

  • Get an at-a-glance view of all your sales activities.
  • Monitor your sales team with an easy-to-read dashboard.
  • Identify and tackle problems as soon as they appear.
