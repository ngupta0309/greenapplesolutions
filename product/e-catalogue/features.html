Product Catalogue App Features

The product catalogue app allows your sales team to use a tablet or smartphone to showcase your products, place orders and submit service requests. Managers can send updates on new launches and also track sales activities.

Showcase your products

  • Showcase your products using a visually-appealing electronic catalogue.
  • Manage 100's or even 1000's of products.
  • Import your products from and Excel file.
  • Update your catalogue from a central web interface. All mobiles and tablets will sync automatically.
  • Add new products or edit your catalogue anytime.

Highlight Images & Videos

  • Manage all your product images and videos from one shared database.
  • Share information about new product releases and upcoming events.

Place orders from anywhere

  • Add items to the order while browsing the product catalogue.
  • Send orders to your office for processing.
  • Manage all your orders information in one place.
  • Update the payment info from any mobile device.

Sales Dashboard

  • Get a snapshot view of your weekly/monthly/quarterly sales.
  • Track real-time sales activities for the entire team.
